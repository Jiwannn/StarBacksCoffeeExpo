App Idea: Coffee Shop Ordering & Management System
Concept Overview:
StarBacks Coffee is a mobile ordering and management application built for a local coffee shop based in Mindanao, Philippines. The app serves two types of users — customers and administrators.
Customers can browse the full coffee menu organized by category (hot coffees, cold coffees, and frappuccinos), customize their orders with options such as size, sugar level, milk type,
and add-ons, add items to their cart, and place orders using either GCash or Cash on Delivery as their payment method.
They can also track the real-time status of their orders from pending all the way to delivered. On the other side, 
administrators have access to a dedicated dashboard where they can monitor sales statistics, manage the product catalog by adding, editing, or toggling product availability,
oversee all incoming orders and update their status through the fulfillment pipeline, and manage registered user accounts. 
The main purpose of StarBacks Coffee is to digitize and streamline the ordering experience for both the customer and the shop owner, reducing manual order-taking, minimizing errors, \
and providing a convenient way for customers to order their favorite coffee from their phone.
